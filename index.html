Oakland County Cleaning FAQs

What is the difference between Pressure Washing and Soft Washing?

Pressure washing uses high-PSI (up to 4200) for hard surfaces like concrete driveways. Soft washing uses specialized chemicals and low pressure to safely clean siding and roofs without damage.

How often should I wash my home in Michigan?

We recommend a professional house wash once a year in Oakland County to remove algae, mold, and salt deposits that accumulate during Michigan winters.
