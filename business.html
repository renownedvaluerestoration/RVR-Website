Driveway Cleaning: 8-Step Professional Process

  1. Site Inspection: Identify cracks, stains, and move obstacles.
  2. Landscape Protection: Pre-wet all surrounding grass and plants.
  3. Surface Pre-Treatment: Apply eco-friendly surfactants to break down oil/mildew.
  4. Detail Edging: Manual high-pressure cleaning of edges and corners.
  5. Surface Cleaning: Professional rotary surface cleaner pass (consistent finish).
  6. Rinse: Thorough flush of all debris to drainage points.
  7. Post-Treatment: Apply "brightener" or sealer if requested by customer.
  8. Final Walkthrough: Ensure site is clean and customer is satisfied.

Chemicals & Safety

We use biodegradable detergents. Always pre-soak vegetation. Use Sodium Hypochlorite (SH) blends only when necessary for organic growth, diluted to safe levels.
